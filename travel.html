About us?

Eight years ago, we started as a small travel agency, taking up five to twenty clients to help them organize their trips to our country, Philippines. Even back then, we always knew our purpose- to give opportunity to all people to have an affordable yet high quality travel experience. Today, we offer more services such as tour packages, customized tours, passport and visa processing, car rentals, and even customized tours. Because of our proven service quality, we managed to have a consistent client base of 100 – 300 persons per year since we launched these new services to the public. We always prioritize to provide tours that could give an amazing experience to our potential customers even without the need of having an expensive budget. We hope that you give us the chance to become your companion in the best travel experience of your life!
